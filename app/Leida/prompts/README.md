# Leida AI Prompts

This folder centralises all AI prompts used by Leida in a single, auditable location.

The system treats prompt content as opaque data — it has no awareness of what the prompts say or mean. This folder exists outside that system boundary so that you, as the founder or business owner, can review and take ownership of the content. This is where professional and legal judgement must be applied: ensuring prompts are accurate, appropriate, and compliant before they are deployed.
